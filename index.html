04 — AI @ Utilities

Dark Software Factory.

The Dark Software Factory is a fast, modern & factory-based cloud software delivery model for German utilities. It’s centered around a lights-out software assembly line with built-in compliance, observability and AI orchestration, supported by Platinion's delivery excellence and deep regulatory know-how.

01

Natively built for grid, meters & compliance

Harmonized domain kit with module templates for grid ops, meter-to-cash, and Trie-basiertes compliance reporting (EnWG, MaBiS, BSI). One codebase, one governance model, built for utility risk profiles.

02

90% pipeline automation & self-remediation

Automated build/test/deploy workflows driven by codified runbooks and AI-driven failure triage; self-healing staging snapshots with built-in drift correction and security as code.

03

Operational security and audit chain

Immutable telemetry from commit-to-postDeploy, auto compliance assertions, and a governance layer aligned with German utility audits (BaFin, TÜV, BSI) for assurance and CISO confidence.

04

Director roadmap: 8 clients in 12 months

Scale through PDCA-connected DSF pods, utility co-innovation labs, and a shared platform core. Target: 1 central factory, 3 satellite delivery lines, 8 anchor accounts by year-end.
